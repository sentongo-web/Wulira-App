# Wulira App - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Wulira ("we," "us," "our," or "App") is an educational language learning application developed by Paul Sentongo. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the Wulira App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### What Data We Collect:
- **Learning Progress Data**: Your lesson completion status, quiz scores, and flashcard reviews (stored locally on your device)
- **User Preferences**: Your selected theme (light/dark), language settings, and app settings
- **Device Information**: Device type, Android version, and app version (for crash reporting only)

### What We DO NOT Collect:
- ❌ Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Audio/Photos from your device
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Payment information (handled by Google Play Store securely)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ User behavior tracking (analytics)

## 3. How We Store Information

All your learning progress and preferences are stored **locally on your device only**. This means:
- ✓ Your data never leaves your phone
- ✓ No data is sent to our servers
- ✓ No cloud syncing (data stays on your device)
- ✓ If you uninstall the app, your local data is deleted
- ✓ You have complete control of your data

## 4. How We Use Information

Your learning progress is used solely for:
1. **Saving Your Progress** - So you can resume where you left off
2. **Tracking Achievement** - Showing you your learning statistics
3. **Personalizing Experience** - Remembering your theme and settings
4. **App Improvement** - Understanding which features you use

## 5. Third-Party Services

### Services Used:
- **Google Play Store**: Handles all payment processing for in-app purchases
  - Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  - No payment data is stored locally

- **Flutter Framework**: Powers the app itself
  - Privacy: https://flutter.dev/docs/development/data-and-backend/state-mgmt
  - No telemetry from Wulira enabled

- **Android OS**: Device-level permissions managed by Android
  - Privacy: https://www.android.com/intl/en_ug/privacy/

### Services NOT Used:
- ❌ Google Analytics
- ❌ Firebase (except if future versions add it explicitly)
- ❌ Third-party ad networks
- ❌ Social media tracking
- ❌ Marketing attribution services

## 6. Permissions We Request

The Wulira App requests the following Android permissions:

### Internet Permission
- **What it does**: Allows the app to connect to the internet
- **Why we need it**: To support potential future online features
- **Current use**: Minimal - mostly offline functionality

### Network State Permission
- **What it does**: Checks if your device is connected to the internet
- **Why we need it**: To determine if online features are available
- **Current use**: Not actively used in v1.0.0

### Audio Recording (if TTS enabled)
- **What it does**: Accesses your device's microphone
- **Why we need it**: For text-to-speech pronunciation features
- **Current use**: Optional - only activated when you enable TTS

## 7. Children's Privacy

**Wulira is suitable for all ages.** The app contains:
- ✓ No violent content
- ✓ No adult material
- ✓ No personal data collection
- ✓ No external links to unsafe sites
- ✓ Educational content only

**We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.** All data storage is local and anonymous.

## 8. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

### Local Storage Security:
- Sensitive data (like preferences) may be encrypted using Android's KeyStore
- Passwords and sensitive values are not stored in plain text
- Database files have appropriate file permissions

### Network Security:
- Any future online communication will use HTTPS encryption
- Certificate pinning may be implemented for sensitive operations

### What You Can Do:
- Store your device in a secure location
- Use Android's built-in screen lock
- Keep your Android OS updated
- Be cautious about sharing your device

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

### Access Your Data:
- All your progress data is stored locally on your device
- You can view it through the app's analytics section

### Delete Your Data:
- Uninstall the app to delete all local data
- Use Android settings to clear app data
- No data is stored on our servers to delete

### Disable Features:
- You can disable TTS/audio features in Settings
- You can switch themes anytime
- You can pause learning anytime

### No Tracking:
- We don't track your behavior across other apps
- We don't create user profiles
- We don't sell or share your data
